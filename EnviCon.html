Past Event Highlight: Climate Engagement Summit (September 30, 2023)

On September 30, 2023, EnviCon took part in an impactful climate engagement summit organised by Southern African Leadership Academy (SALA) that focused on driving community-led solutions for a sustainable future. The event brought together young changemakers, local leaders, and environmental advocates to share ideas, collaborate, and spark grassroots action. It was a day filled with insight, energy, and momentum toward climate resilience.
